ABOUT US



Static Movement Sound consists of professional DJ’s who have been performing for over a decade We take pride in providing music in various settings such as, weddings, cooperate or private events, nightclubs, wineries and even party barges! Reggae, dance hall, afro beats, hip hop, R&B and SOCA are our primary genre of music but are able to take requests and individualize playlists as needed.
You can find us performing locally in the Okanagan Valley of British Columbia and the Peace Region of Alberta as well as internationally throughout the Caribbean,
North American and parts of Europe. As musicians we are dedicated to our craft and passionate about making people dance. As the great Bob Marley said “when music hits, you feel no pain”
